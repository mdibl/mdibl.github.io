# Introduction to Data Science

This series of workshops is designed for undergraduate researchers and students who are new to working with biological data. No programming experience is required to start — each module builds on the previous one, taking you from raw spreadsheet data through reproducible analysis and visualization.

## Who This Series Is For

These workshops are designed for undergraduate students, early-stage graduate students, and researchers who work with biological data but have not yet had formal training in data management or computational analysis. If you have ever struggled with a messy spreadsheet, wondered how to share data with a collaborator, or wanted to make a figure in R — this series is for you.

## How to Use This Series

Work through the modules in order — each one builds on skills from the previous. The course pages stay available after in-person sessions so you can revisit any section, finish exercises at your own pace, and bring any questions you may have to the CGDS Core office hours.
